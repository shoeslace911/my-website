About me

I am Sho Fujiwara and as of 2022 have been coding and designing for a year and a half. I have done a lot of jobs from waiter, to a clerk to a bartender but never in design and knew about coding and computers outside video games.

After a year of deciding what to do with the rest of my life I tried my hand on web design and graphics design and had a blast with it because of all the freedom, all the colors and all the beautiful designs one can do in this field. I then decided I wanted to be a software developer. I also took photography classes and love to take random pictures for picture perfect scenarios, or sometimes random things.

I specialize in Frontend and Graphics design with emphasis on friendly and nice looking UI.
